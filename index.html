Confirm Button

ConfirmButton is a jQuery-UI plugin that creates a modal button useful for making it hard to perform a destructive action by accident without getting in the way.

A Quick Demo

Instructions

  1. Click the button.
  2. Now click away
  3. Now click the button again
  4. Click the confirm button once
  5. View the source


$('#quick-demo-button').confirmButton({
	 button : {label: 'Remove Me'}
	})
	.bind('confirmedclick', function(){
		// on confirmed click hide the button for 10 seconds
		$('#quick-demo-button').fadeOut()
		setTimeout(function(){
			$('#quick-demo-button').fadeIn();
		}, 10000);
});
